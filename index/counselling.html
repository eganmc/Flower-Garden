Safeguarding Counselling Program

Counselling Information

Our referral program connects individuals experiencing a need for safeguarding with experienced counsellors who can provide support, guidance, and a safe space to talk. Whether you are seeking help for yourself or referring someone you care about, our team is here to listen and assist every step of the way.

We understand that reaching out can be difficult, and pride ourselves in being gentle and respectful in all aspects of our program. We would ask that, if you are reaching out on behalf of another person, that they are present and ready to provide their details or the details of a close family member if they are otherwise unavailable.

Your privacy is our priority. All information shared with us is protected under our strict privacy policy and will never be disclosed. We are committed to safeguarding both you and your personal details throughout your journey.
