About Us

Flower Garden began as a simple concept, a resource for people to educate themselves on Safeguarding practices so they could help others.

However, the more we shared and the more we talked, we realised we could have a very real and personal effect on those individuals without hoping someone else was watching.

As a result, Flower Garden evolved into a sanctuary, a place where vulnerable people could share their stories and approach our counsel themselves, or with the help of someone who cares about them a lot.

We're here to help in any way we can, and in whatever capacity you require for your own journey. Whether you are seeking advice, inspiration, or simply a place to connect, you are always welcome in our garden.
